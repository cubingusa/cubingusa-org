CubingUSA Nationals 2019

We're pleased to announce that CubingUSA Nationals 2019 will be held July 25-28, 2019 at the Baltimore Convention Center in Baltimore, Maryland!

CubingUSA Nationals 2019 will be the 15th US National Championship. Each year, the competition brings together hundreds of the top American speedcubers, as well as many international competitors, for a fun weekend of solving puzzles.

Nationals is the largest and most professional speedcubing competition in the USA each year, brought to you by a dedicated team of volunteers who work year-round to support our community. This year's Nationals will be the largest speedcubing competition in the USA to date.

This year, for the first time, Nationals will be held over four days (Thursday to Sunday). We're looking forward to a long weekend full of cubing!

Key Facts

Dates: July 25-28, 2019
Location: Baltimore, Maryland
Venue: Baltimore Convention Center
Prizes: To be announced

Registration

Registration will take place on the WCA website. There are qualifying times in all events; you must qualify in at least one event to register. Spectators do not need to register.

Limit: 1100 competitors
Qualifying Times: See events page.
You can get a 50% refund for cancelling until the refund deadline, after which we will no longer give refunds.

  • June 18: Deadline for qualifying times
  • June 24: Last day to modify registration
  • July 25-28: Competition!
