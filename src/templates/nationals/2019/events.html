CubingUSA Nationals 2019 Events

All events have qualifying times, which must be set in other WCA competitions. You must qualify for at least one event in order to register for the competition. After registration closes, we will remove competitors from all events in which they are not qualified. It is your responsibility to ensure that you are qualified before registering.

Qualifying times listed are single for all blindfolded events, and average for all other events. All qualifying times are "less than or equal to". For example, a competitor with a 30.00 second average in 3x3x3 would qualify.
