New England Cubing

Welcome!

New England has a vibrant cubing community with competitions happening regularly in many areas. We are focused on facilitating enjoyable competitions in the region, and bringing events to new regions. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please contact newengland@cubingusa.org. If you're interested in having a competition in your area, please take a look at the information for organizers page.
