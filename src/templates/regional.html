CubingUSA Regional Championships

In addition to CubingUSA Nationals, CubingUSA supports the organization of seven Regional Championships each year. These competitions aim to provide competitors throughout the US with a high-quality, large competition experience, and to recognize the top competitors throughout the country. Read More...

Just like CubingUSA Nationals, our Regional Championships are open to all competitors. However, the title of Regional Champion will be awarded to the top-ranked competitor who lives in that region. In order to be eligible to win a title, you need to log in to the CubingUSA website with your WCA account and select your home state before the competition.

Learn more about our Regional Championship Title Rules.
